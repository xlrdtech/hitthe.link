About the Hall

An elegant address for South Fulton's most important days

Set on Roosevelt Highway in Union City, South Fulton Event Hall is a versatile, all-inclusive venue made for the moments that matter — weddings, milestone birthdays, sweet sixteens, corporate gatherings, and the celebrations in between.

With a spacious hall, on-site chair and throne rentals, a mobile photo booth, and ample parking, everything you need to host beautifully lives in one place. Open seven days a week, with a team known for being patient, present, and genuinely eager to help.
