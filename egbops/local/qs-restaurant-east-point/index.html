The Kitchen

Real Southern Cooking,
Served Generous.

Q's Restaurant is East Point's downtown soul-food kitchen — a cafeteria line where the steam table is loaded fresh every morning and the plates come out the way grandma made them. Just off the East Point MARTA station on White Way, it's where neighbors, commuters, and whole families pull up a tray for turkey wings, oxtails, fried pork chops, and all the sides that matter.

No shortcuts, no frozen freight — just real Southern cooking, served generous and served daily.
