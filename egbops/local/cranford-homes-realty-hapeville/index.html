About Cranford

Cranford Homes & Realty Co. is Hapeville's hometown brokerage, working the airport-adjacent Tri-Cities market for nearly three decades from its office on North Central Avenue. With 290+ closings on record, the firm has built its name on single-family home sales, buyer representation, and owner-side leasing across Hapeville, College Park, and East Point. Clients work directly with people who know these neighborhoods street by street — the schools, the commute to Hartsfield-Jackson, the blocks where values are climbing. Whether you are listing a longtime home, buying your first, or placing a rental, Cranford brings local roots and real market depth to the table.
