Our Mission

“To provide accessible locations for public service activities in the metropolitan Atlanta communities for youth and adults.”

We concentrate our efforts in areas with limited resources — meeting people where they are, and walking with them toward something better. From a young person reaching for college to a senior planning a secure retirement, Fortitude is built to lift whole lives.

What We Do

Three pillars. One promise.

Who We Are

A brand as resilient as the families it lifts.

The Fortitude Educational and Cultural Development Center is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving metropolitan Atlanta. We provide accessible public-service programming for both youth and seniors — pairing scholarship, financial literacy, health, and personal development for the next generation with retirement, housing, and financial-literacy education for our elders.

Guided by President Minnie Miller and a dedicated board, we focus our energy where resources are thin and the need is real — because fortitude isn't a slogan here. It's how we show up, every day, for whole children and whole seniors alike.
