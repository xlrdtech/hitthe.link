◆ The House ◆

Diamond Cutz Studios is the home of Stal'lyon's signature sound — a Southern Soul recording and live-entertainment house rooted right here in Atlanta. We build records from the ground up: recording, producing, mixing, and mastering with that hyper-real shine and a guitar-meets-808 groove you can feel. From the studio booth to the live stage, the DJ booth to the dance floor, we bring the soul for all your music and entertainment needs. Pull up — let's cut something timeless.

◆ The Music ◆

Stal'lyon

Andre — known on stage as Stal'lyon — is a Southern Soul artist out of Atlanta with a sound all his own: gritty guitar riffs riding hard over knocking 808s. Charismatic, authentic, built for the stage, he turns every set into a celebration while keeping the grown-folks soul tradition alive for a new generation.
