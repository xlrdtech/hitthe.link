Infrastructure Call

  • Hetzner RAM: 64 GB — plenty. RAM is not the constraint.
  • Hetzner storage: none. Matrix/Beeper eats disk, not RAM — a homeserver with no disk crashes on media/history growth.
  • Decision: Beeper-hosted does the bridging on their disk → pipe into our client. No paid Hetzner volume (extra cost, breaks free-only). Sovereignty lives in the client we own, not in self-hosting storage.
  • Self-host on a volume only as a fallback if Beeper-hosted ever fails. M4 is out (no local Docker).
