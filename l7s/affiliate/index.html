Program Overview

What the partnership is, and who it's built for.

The L7S Affiliate & Strategic Partnership Program lets trusted partners refer clients into the L7S service tracks — credit, cashflow, capital, and the rest of the seven C's — and earn for every referral that converts and stays. You bring the relationship; L7S delivers the work and handles fulfillment end to end.

It's a clean, transparent arrangement: you refer, the client onboards through L7S, and you're paid on the engagement. No inventory, no fulfillment burden, no support tickets on your side — your only job is the introduction.

02

How It Works

Three moves from introduction to payout.
1
You Refer
Introduce a prospect from your network and point them to the right L7S track. Your referral is tagged to you from first contact.
2
Client Onboards
L7S takes it from there — consultation, scoping, onboarding, and full delivery. You're kept in the loop; we carry the work.
3
You Earn
When the client activates and stays, you're paid on the engagement per your partner tier — including recurring tracks where they apply.

Every referral is attributed to the partner who brought it in, tracked through onboarding, and reconciled against the agreed structure. Payout cadence and rail are confirmed with you on activation.
