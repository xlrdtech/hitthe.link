Know Your Numbers

The five KPIs every Cashflow Hero tracks. Master these and you run the business — it doesn't run you.
Freedom #
Freedom Number
The monthly revenue that covers your cost of living + cost of business + profit goal. Your single north-star target.
BEP
Break-Even Point
The revenue where total income equals total cost — $0 profit. The floor you must never fall below.
KPIs
Key Performance Indicators
The handful of numbers you check weekly — clients closed, revenue, pace vs. target — so nothing drifts.
LTV
Lifetime Value
Total revenue one client brings over the whole relationship. Higher LTV means fewer clients to hit Freedom.
CAC
Customer Acquisition Cost
What it costs to win one client. Keep CAC well under LTV and every new client compounds your profit.
