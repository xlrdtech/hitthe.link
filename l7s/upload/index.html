◆ How your files are transferred

This page does not store or upload your documents — and it intentionally never asks you to type sensitive numbers like a full SSN or account number. It collects your intake status and saves it on this device only. When you tap Submit Intake, your status is compiled into a single message to East at east@xlrd.org. East then sends you a private, encrypted upload link where your actual files are transferred securely. Your documents are handled with encryption and used only for your engagement — never sold or shared.
