The flow

Four steps from commitment to kickoff.

A repeatable system — so every client is onboarded the same clean way, every time.

01

Pay securely

You receive a secure checkout link for your agreed offer. Pay by card — confirmation is instant and logged.

02

Once payment, agreement, and documents are verified, we activate your service and confirm your kickoff call.
