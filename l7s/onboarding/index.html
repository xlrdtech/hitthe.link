SOP

The 7-Step Onboarding Pipeline

From commitment to kickoff — the exact intake sequence.
1
Prospect says “Yes”
Confirm in writing that the prospect is ready to move forward with the agreed [Offer] at the agreed [Price]. A clear written yes is what starts the clock — no ambiguity, no verbal-only commitments.
2
Send Stripe Checkout link
Share the v1 payment link for the agreed offer and confirm once payment clears. Card payment is secure and confirmation is instant.
Stripe Checkout link & payment confirmation plug in here
3
Payment confirmation
Once paid, log the client in Asana under “Client Onboarding v1” and tag East / Luckie. This creates the tracked record so the rest of the pipeline has a single source of truth.
Asana “Client Onboarding v1” · tag East / Luckie
4
Send agreement + intake form
Send the branded service agreement together with a fillable intake form for the client to review, complete, and sign. Delivered as a PDF or via e-signature.
DocuSign agreement + fillable intake (PDF / e-sign) plug in here
5
Send secure uploader link
Send the client a secure uploader link with the list of required documents — ID, SSN, bank statements, and any other documents specific to the offer. Sensitive documents go through the encrypted portal, never over text or email.
Secure Uploader encrypted document portal link plugs in here
6
Verify documents & forms
Confirm all three are in: payment received, agreement signed, and documents uploaded. When every box is checked, log the client as “Complete”.
Payment received Agreement signed Documents uploaded Log “Complete”
7
Activate service & archive final
Move the client to the correct board, save an S.O.S. Snapshot of the offer, scope, and status, and schedule the kickoff. The client is now live and the record is archived for reference.
Move to correct board S.O.S. Snapshot · offer / scope / status Schedule kickoff
