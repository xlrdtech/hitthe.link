Scripts

Short, reusable community posts and DMs — written clean and on-brand. Keep them simple, keep them consistent.
◆ Editable starting templates — copy, swap the highlighted placeholders for your specifics, then post. Keep one voice across all of them.
Welcome Post
Pinned to the feed · greets every new member
Welcome to the L7S community 👋 You're in the right room. This is where we keep it simple and actually move — Credit, Cashflow, and the steps to your Freedom Number. Three things to start: 1. Head to Start Here and watch the quick orientation. 2. Drop a comment below — your first name, where you're from, and the ONE win you want first. 3. Explore the modules — Credit Hero and Cashflow Hero are your guided paths. We win together here. Glad you're with us. — [Your name], L7S
Weekly Check-In Prompt
Same day & time each week · drives engagement
Weekly Check-In 🔥 Quick one to keep us accountable: • What's ONE win from this week — big or small? • What's the ONE thing you're focused on next week? • Where are you stuck? (Ask here — that's what we're for.) Reply in the comments. Reading every one. Let's keep the momentum going. — [Your name]
Offer / CTA Post
When promoting a program or next step
Ready for the next step? 🚀 If you've been working through the modules and want the guided path, [program name] is open. Here's what you get: • [Benefit 1] • [Benefit 2] • [Benefit 3] Who it's for: [ideal member]. Want in? Comment "[keyword]" below or tap the link here 👉 [link] Simple as that. Let's get you the result. — [Your name]

Sent 1:1 right after a member joins

Talk soon. — [Your name], L7S
