Lead-Ready Standards

What it takes to receive leads.

Leads are dispatched only to contractors who are ready to represent the brand and treat every customer right. Meeting these standards is how we protect your reputation — and ours.

01 / APPEARANCE

Company-branded apparel

Show up representing the company — a legitimate branded shirt or uniform and a professional appearance on every job. The look has to match the brand the lead trusted.

02 / AGREEMENT

Signed program agreement

An active, signed program agreement and up-to-date contract on file. This confirms you're in the program and cleared to receive dispatched leads.

03 / PROTOCOL

Follow the lead-handling protocol

Pitch correctly and treat every lead like a real customer — appearance, materials, and conduct all consistent with the brand from first contact to close.

⚠️

Lead-ready means lead-ready. No branded apparel, no signed agreement, or not following the protocol means leads aren't dispatched. The standards exist to keep every customer experience — and your conversion rate — high.
