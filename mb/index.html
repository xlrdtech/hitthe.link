◐ The Story ◐

The bio · in his own line

Benjamin McAllister, better known as MB, is an independent artist, vocalist, and songwriter out of Douglasville, Georgia — carving out his own lane in today's music scene. With a voice that blends raw emotion and soulful depth, he delivers music that connects across genres and cultures. His sound is rooted in R&B, hip-hop, and southern soul, creating a signature style that's both modern and timeless.

Passionate about authenticity, MB pours real-life experiences into his lyrics, giving listeners songs that feel genuine and relatable. Known for catchy hooks, smooth delivery, and undeniable stage presence, he continues to build a reputation as an artist who can move both the heart and the crowd.

His latest single, "Trail Ride (feat. Stal'lyon)," showcases his versatility and collaborative spirit — combining southern soul flavor with infectious energy that resonates with fans everywhere.

MB is more than just a performer — he's a storyteller, bringing people together through music that speaks to life, love, and the culture of the South. With growing momentum and a dedicated fan base, he's ready to take his artistry to the next level.
