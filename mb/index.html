About M_B

Real Southern Soul, hand-cut out of Douglasville, GA.

Live Vocals, Every Show

No backing tracks pretending to be live. M_B sings the show — trail rides, field parties, rodeos, clubs, festivals. The crowd gets the real thing.

The Sound

Southern Soul with the bones of R&B and hip-hop. Made for stomping, two-stepping, and singing along — line dancers, riders, and the Mane Family Mafia all eat.

The Crew

Mane Family Mafia rolls with M_B — the Steppaz, the riders, the family. Bookings handled direct. Contracts, riders, sound — all squared away before show day.
