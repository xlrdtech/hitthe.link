How we got this answer
Your cohort median is modeled from age-bracket FICO medians, adjusted by an income-tier modifier and a US Census division offset. Your percentile is estimated from a normal distribution around that median (σ ≈ 58 pts), and the top-decile target sits ~1.28σ above it. The gauge plots your score on the canonical FICO scale (Poor 300–579 · Fair 580–669 · Good 670–739 · Very Good 740–799 · Exceptional 800–850).
