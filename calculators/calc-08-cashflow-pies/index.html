ⓘ How we score this · methodology & assumptions ›

The P.I.E.S.™ score rewards Profit and Savings while penalizing heavy Expense, using (Profit×2 + Savings×2 + (100 − Expense)) ÷ 5, clamped to 0–100. Tiers: Fragile below 10, Building 10–15, Solid 16–21, Strong 22–27, Elite 28+.

Channel benchmarks: Profit Strong ≥ 10%, Savings Strong ≥ 15%, Expense Lean < 70%. Profit, Expense, and Savings always rebalance to total 100% of income.

This is an educational allocation estimate — not financial advice, and running it commits you to nothing. Numbers update live as you adjust; nothing is stored or sent.
