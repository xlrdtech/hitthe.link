How we score this

Benchmarks. Each category is graded against a recommended share of after-tax income: Housing 30%, Transportation 15%, Food 15%, Healthcare 10%, Debt Service 15%, Lifestyle 10%, Other 5%. Within benchmark = OK, up to 25% over = Watch, beyond that = Over.

Health score. 10 points for each of the 7 categories kept within benchmark (up to 70), plus up to 30 points scaled by your savings rate (full 30 at a 30%+ savings rate). Capped at 100.

Estimate, not advice. This is an educational planning tool. Nothing is stored, nothing is shared, and running it won't affect anything but your insight.

Calculations run entirely in your browser. No data leaves this page.
