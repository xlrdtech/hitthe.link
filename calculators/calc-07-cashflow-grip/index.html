⌁The Four Tiers · Earn-While-You-Learn Ladder

  1. Affiliate Partnership Program Access — open-door entry. Anyone can refer and earn per attributed conversion. Lowest commitment, lowest share.
  2. 1099 SE · IC Reseller (Franchise) — Independent Contractor with their own book of business. Self-employed, manages their own taxes, commission scales.
  3. ISO · White-Label · Licensed Seat — Independent Sales Organization with EULA + SULA license. Can White-Label or Grey-Label the offering. Authorized partner status.
  4. Enterprise · JO · JV · BCA — Joint Operations, Joint Ventures, Business Collaboration Agreements. The L7X model. Largest share, deepest commitment.
