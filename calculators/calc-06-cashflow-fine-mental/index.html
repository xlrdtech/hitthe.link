How this readout is calculated

Net Worth = total Assets − total Liabilities. Blank or invalid entries count as $0; negative figures are clamped to $0.

Assets share = assets ÷ (assets + liabilities). A healthy position trends toward a larger gold ring and a positive net worth.

Trajectory is a straight projection of your own input: future net worth = today's net worth + (monthly amount you set aside × number of months). No growth rate, no market returns, no interest are assumed or fabricated. Enter $0 and the line stays flat — we never invent growth you didn't put in. Illustrative only; real life varies.
