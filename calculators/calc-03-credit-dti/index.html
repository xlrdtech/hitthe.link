How we got this answer ▾

Front-end DTI = housing payment ÷ gross monthly income. Lenders want this ≤ 28%.

Back-end DTI = all monthly debt (housing + auto + cards + student + personal + other) ÷ gross monthly income. Conforming loans target ≤ 36%; the Qualified Mortgage (QM) rule caps most loans at 43%.

We apply the recognized 28 / 36 rule and the federal QM threshold to flag your bucket and the exact paydown needed to move up.

Estimates for planning only — not a credit decision. Running this does not affect your credit score. Lender overlays, compensating factors, and loan program vary; confirm with your loan officer.
