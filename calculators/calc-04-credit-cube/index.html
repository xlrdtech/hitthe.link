⚑ The C.U.B.E. Protocol · 14 Steps
  1. 1Activate or login to preferred credit monitoring service.
  2. 2Pull 3-Bureau Tri-Merge Credit Report (Experian · Equifax · TransUnion) as PDF.
  3. 3Locate FICO Score Ingredients: Payment History (35%), Credit Utilization (30%), Length of Credit (15%), Credit Mix (10%), New Credit (10%).
  4. 4Focus on Credit Utilization (30%, "Amount of Debt") — revolving tradelines only.
  5. 5⚠️ Revolving = credit cards only. NOT mortgages, auto loans, personal loans.
  6. 6Find utilization % for each individual credit card.
  7. 7Find 3 key dates per tradeline: Statement Cycle Date, Due Date, Report Date.
  8. 8Use a physical calendar with blue ink to mark all 3 dates for each main card.
  9. 9Mirror to digital calendar + reminders.
  10. 10Calculate 7-9% range of credit limit for your top 3 primary tradelines.
  11. 11Build payment schedule to keep balance in 7-9% range (under 10% = excellent).
  12. 12Make 3 to 5 payments every 2 days (network processing time 2-3 business days).
  13. 13Have a card for beginning, middle, and end of the month.
  14. 14Move balances using the Captain of the C's Codex — Credit · Cash · Crypto · Currency · Conversions X-Change Method. Automate the habit.
