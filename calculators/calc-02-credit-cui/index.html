How we got this answer ▾
FICO & VantageScore aligned Revolving utilization model

Aggregate CUI = total balances ÷ total credit limits, expressed as a percent. Per-card utilization uses the same ratio for each line. Lines with no limit entered are excluded from the aggregate.

Scoring tiers mirror lender guidance: <1% best, <10% excellent, <30% good, ≥30% warning, ≥50% red flag. The 30% mark is the widely-cited scoring cliff. Both overall and per-card utilization matter, so we surface each.

This is an educational estimate to help you plan paydowns — running it never affects your real credit score and nothing is stored.
