London Day Break

Curtis Henderson's GBP-cross breakout system. Price the 2-3 AM London open, place an OCO pair of pending orders, let the market pick a side.

Strategy Rules (per Curtis)

  1. Mark the 2 AM candle high and low (London pre-open).
  2. Place a BUY-STOP 10 pips above the high.
  3. Place a SELL-STOP 5 pips below the low.
  4. Stop loss: 30 pips from entry. Take profit: 25 pips (or 20 if previous candles were large).
  5. Once one side triggers, cancel the opposite order immediately.

Inputs

Industry standard: 1-2% max
Curtis default: 25 (or 20 if large prior candles)
