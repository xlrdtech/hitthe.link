How we calculate this ▾
Each period the balance is multiplied by (1 + growth rate) and any per-period contribution is added, then compounded again the next period. When a contribution frequency differs from the growth period, we spread it proportionally across periods. These figures are educational estimates of compounding mechanics, not a forecast of investment returns or a guarantee — sustained high per-period rates are exceptionally rare. Run different scenarios freely; nothing is stored and no sign-up is required.
