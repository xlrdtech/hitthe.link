// Straight answers

The questions everyone asks first.

Will callers know it's not a live person?

The whole point is that it answers as you, in your tone, and can actually act — book, quote a range, route. Most callers just feel taken care of. If you ever want it disclosed, we set that too. You're in control of what it says.

Does it use my real phone number?

Yes — we point your existing business line at the agent, or set up a dedicated number that forwards. Either way, the caller reaches your business and hears your business.

Can it book straight into my calendar?

Yes. It reads your live availability, offers real open slots, books the appointment, and sends a confirmation. It only ever books times you actually have free.

What if it doesn't know an answer?

It's built with your real answers and a clear rule for everything else: capture the details, then either text you or warm-transfer a live person. It never makes things up and never dead-ends the caller.

How fast can it be live?

The First Win can be standing up in days, not months — fast enough to hear yourself answer this week. The fuller builds add integration time, scoped on your call.

What exactly does the monthly cover?

Keeping the agent live, answering, hosted, and improving — plus the tuning as your business changes. The exact monthly is confirmed on your setup call against your real call volume and scope. We quote real numbers, never placeholders.
