Stal'lyon — The Artist

ANDRE' JERMAINE, professionally known as STAL' LYON, is emerging as one of the most captivating new voices in modern Southern Soul. Born and raised in Detroit, Michigan, the legendary home of Motown, STAL' LYON is bringing a fresh, powerful, and genre-defying sound to audiences across the country.

Rooted in the rich traditions of Motown while embracing the heartfelt storytelling of Southern Soul, STAL' LYON has crafted a musical identity that is uniquely his own. His sound effortlessly blends Soul, R&B, Southern Soul, Pop, and Rock influences, creating music that resonates across generations and musical tastes.

What makes STAL' LYON stand apart is his extraordinary versatility. A gifted vocalist, songwriter, arranger, performer, vocal coach, and creative visionary, he refuses to be limited by any one genre or style. His ability to transition seamlessly from smooth, soulful melodies to powerful, high-energy performances showcases a level of artistry rarely found in today's music landscape.

Known for his distinctive vocal tone, emotional delivery, and commanding stage presence, STAL' LYON creates more than songs, he creates experiences. Whether performing heartfelt ballads, Southern Soul anthems, crossover hits, commercial jingles, or inspirational music, his passion for connecting with people remains at the center of every performance.

With Detroit's musical excellence in his foundation and Southern Soul flowing through his artistry, STAL' LYON represents the next evolution of soul music. His commitment to authenticity, creativity, and musical excellence continues to attract attention from fans, promoters, and industry professionals alike.

As Southern Soul continues its rise across the nation, STAL' LYON stands ready to leave an undeniable mark on the industry. With new music, live performances, and exciting projects on the horizon, he is proving that true artistry knows no boundaries.
