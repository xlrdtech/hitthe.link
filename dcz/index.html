The Artist

Stal'lyon

Andre Jermaine, professionally known as Stal'lyon, is one of the most captivating new voices in modern Southern Soul. Born and raised in Detroit, the legendary home of Motown, he now cuts records out of Diamond Cutz Studios in Atlanta.

Rooted in Motown tradition and the heartfelt storytelling of Southern Soul, Stal'lyon blends Soul, R&B, Pop and Rock into a sound that's all his own. Music that resonates across generations. A gifted vocalist, songwriter, arranger, performer and vocal coach, he refuses to be limited by any one genre.

Known for his distinctive tone, emotional delivery and commanding stage presence, Stal'lyon doesn't just make songs, he makes experiences. From soulful ballads to high-energy anthems, his passion for connecting with people is at the center of every performance.
