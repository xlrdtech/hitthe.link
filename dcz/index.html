The House

Southern Soul, cut in Atlanta

Diamond Cutz Studios is the home of Stal'lyon's signature sound — a Southern Soul recording and live-entertainment house rooted in Atlanta. We build records from the ground up: recording, producing, mixing, and mastering with that hyper-real shine and a guitar-meets-808 groove you can feel. From the studio booth to the live stage, the DJ booth to the dance floor, we bring the soul. Pull up — let's cut something timeless.
