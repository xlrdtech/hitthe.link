What E.A.T.S. actually means09 / 11
The acronym, decoded

Four letters. Four jobs.

E
Essential
The data that actually matters to the patient: labs, nutrition, budget, access. Filtered, not flooded.
A
AI
Interprets lab values, matches them to food categories, builds plans, ranks swaps. Curated index, not free-text guessing.
T
Tracking
Every scan rolls into a trend. Every gap becomes a meal. Every meal becomes a grocery list.
S
System
Lab portals, scanners, food databases, local logistics — aggregated into one mobile surface a patient can use in the aisle.
