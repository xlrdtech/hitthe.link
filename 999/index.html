The Artist

Esoteric urban music · the 999 brand

The esoteric urban music artist — trap, gangsta, southern, experimental. Themes of duality, metaphysics, spiritual warfare, ego-death and Christ-consciousness. The Purgatory Series

Concept

Purgatory is XLRD999's recurring body of work — the descent that continues. Each release is a full project, not a single: the in-between state where ego dies, the war between flesh and spirit plays out, and meaning is rebuilt line by line through paradox and esoteric symbol.

The active chapter is Purgatory 6 (P6.5), arriving 10.13.2026. The series is the canvas; X9 is increasingly the brush — generating the sonics to XLRD999's spec while the artist owns the vision and the curation.
