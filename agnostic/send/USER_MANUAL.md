# User Manual: FastShare PWA

## Getting Started
FastShare is a lightweight utility for sending files to other apps via your browser.

### 1. Adding Files
- **Drag & Drop:** Drag files from your computer and drop them into the dashed box.
- **Browse:** Click anywhere inside the dashed box to open your system's file explorer.

### 2. Managing the List
- **Remove File:** Click the red **X** next to any file in the list to remove it.
- **Clear All:** Click the **Clear All** button at the top of the list to remove all selected files.

### 3. Sharing
- Click the large blue **Share Files** button.
- Your device's native share menu will appear.
- Select the target application (e.g., WhatsApp, Gmail, Slack) to complete the transfer.

### 4. Installation (PWA)
- **Desktop:** Look for the "Install" icon in your browser's address bar or click the **Install App** button in the FastShare navigation bar.
- **Mobile:** Tap the **Install App** button or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" menu option.
- Once installed, the app will work offline and appear in your app drawer/start menu.

---
**Note:** The share feature requires a browser that supports the Web Share API (Chrome 75+, Edge 81+, Safari 12.1+).
