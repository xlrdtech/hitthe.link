// 03 · Payment lane

One real firm rail.

Stripe is the one canonical payment lane.

Replaces ad-hoc Cash App / Venmo / one-off invoices. Makes Executive Leverage look like a real firm, not a side hustle.
  • Invoicing: Stripe Invoices · branded · Net 7 default · auto-reminders · receipts emailed
  • Padre $2K closes: Stripe Payment Link delivered via SMS (the Padre call script gets the link, not Cash App)
  • Recurring monthly fees: Stripe Subscriptions · maintenance plans · auto-charge on cycle
  • Deposit-first deals: Stripe Checkout split — 50% on signature, 50% on delivery
  • Cash App / Venmo / PayPal: deprecated for client billing · personal-only or rare partner transfers (Anthony V Loopback)
  • Receipts/tax: Stripe → QuickBooks sync · single audit trail
