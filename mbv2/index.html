◐ The Story

The bio · in his own line

Benjamin McAllister, better known as M_B, is an independent artist, vocalist, and songwriter out of Douglasville, Georgia — carving out his own lane in today's music scene. With a voice that blends raw emotion and soulful depth, he delivers music that connects across genres and cultures.

His sound is rooted in R&B, hip-hop, and southern soul — creating a signature style that's both modern and timeless. Passionate about authenticity, M_B pours real-life experiences into his lyrics, giving listeners songs that feel genuine and relatable.

"More than a performer — a storyteller bringing people together through music that speaks to life, love, and the culture of the South."

His latest single, "Trail Ride (feat. Stal'lyon)," showcases his versatility and collaborative spirit — combining southern soul flavor with infectious energy that resonates with fans everywhere. With growing momentum and a dedicated fan base, he's ready to take his artistry to the next level.
