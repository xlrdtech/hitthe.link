(FAQ)

NEED

ANSWER

Whether you're planning your first record or refining the M_B® catalog vision — here are answers to common questions about how we work.

What type of artists do you work with?
Independent artists, festival circuit acts, and brand collaborations. Trail, R&B, soul, bounce, live performance lanes specifically.
Do you offer full-service production?
Yes — from intake to final mastered & distributed record. Mix, master, cover art, distribution upload, sync ready.
Can we book just one service, like mixing or cover art?
Yes. Individual services are available; reach out via the contact line for à la carte pricing.
How far in advance should we book?
4–6 weeks for single release, 8–12 weeks for project cohort. Festival booking lead-time longer.
Do you work internationally?
Yes — we're based in Celina, GA but the catalog reaches globally via DistroKid, Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, SoundCloud, BJR.
What's your pricing structure?
Two tiers — Single Release and Project Cohort. Custom scope quotes available. Payments via PayPal or Square.
