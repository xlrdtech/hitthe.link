5 What the Beside Receptionist Adds

Filling the gaps the current site leaves open

The practice today has no phone line and no online booking — patients can only email or submit a form and wait. The AI receptionist closes that loop.

Phone coverage

A live line that answers every call, so no patient reaches a dead end — the single biggest gap on the site today.

Appointment capture

Takes appointment requests by voice, qualifies them, and captures name, callback, and reason — the exact intent the on-site form collects, handled in real time.

Calendar booking

Optionally books straight into a calendar once Andre provides one, turning a request into a confirmed slot.

Insurance & FAQ answers

Answers the common questions already on the site — specialties served, insurance accepted, how to send documents to FCSALLC@gmail.com.

The core value: the site says "please send us a message, or call us for an appointment" — but there is no number to call and no way to book. The receptionist becomes that number and that booking path.
