04M4 disk fault — the real find

disk8 (the M4 volume) is dropping APFS transaction flushes to 43–221ms, with 100+ operations queued behind each one.

Chased the @self login timeout through three layers before finding the actual cause: it isn't Cloudflare, it isn't Synapse, it's the physical/external M4 drive itself.

kernel: (apfs) tx_flush:1344: disk8 xid 8399232 tx stats:
  # 6220 owait 105  # 105 ops waiting on this flush
  115709us          # ~116ms wait
  flush 221235us     # 221ms to actually flush

That pattern repeats every 40–60 seconds. It explains everything downstream: Synapse's restarted process sat in uninterruptible I/O sleep (STAT: U) for 10+ minutes without even opening its database file, system load sat at 7–10 with 16 processes stuck, and even lsof and ps aux started timing out — a sign the kernel's own I/O layer was under real strain, not just CPU contention.
