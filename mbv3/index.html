(05) FAQ

Frequently
Asked
Questions

What's the turnaround for a custom record?

4–8 weeks for production-to-master, longer if it's a full project. M_B never ships a record that isn't ready.

Can M_B perform at trail rides?

Yes — direct booking through M_B Studio. Trail rides, private events, festivals. Travel arranged from East Allen.

Where can I stream the catalog?

Every major platform — Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Tidal, SoundCloud, Amazon. Search "M_B" or any track title above.

Does M_B accept features and collabs?

Yes — selectively. Existing collabs: Stallyon (Trail Ride, OK), more in the pipeline. Send tracks via the form below.

Sync licensing for film / TV / brand?

Open. M_B owns all masters and publishing — direct deals, no middle-man clearance delays. Use the form to inquire.
