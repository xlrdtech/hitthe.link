How it works. Names are generated in your browser from your idea using alliteration, portmanteau, rhyme, clipping, vowel-drop, affixation, compounding, reduplication, number-glyphs (angel numbers), and more. Free picks are real subdomains on Public-Suffix providers (Cloudflare Pages, Vercel, Netlify, GitHub, js.org, is-a.dev, eu.org). Owned picks use Spaceship's cheap TLDs — a companion checker (scope-watch/domain-finder) verifies live availability + excludes premium via the Spaceship API (the key stays server-side); verified-available results load from available.json, and each links straight to checkout where the live price (typically <$10 on these TLDs) shows. No fabricated prices.
