③ Call flow · ELSO Mode

Arm: call East AI → say "ELSO <lead>" (e.g. "ELSO Cranford"). Owner-only phrase — never spoken to the lead.
  1. Instant pickup — dial the East-AI line from its own number; it answers with no ring.
  2. Inject "ELSO <lead>" — loads that lead's card (business, city, site-status → opener, preview URL).
  3. Dash — East AI stays silent until the bridged lead speaks, then opens as East with the status-matched line.
  4. Gauge in <5 min — fast hot/cold read. Hard 10-min cap.
  5. Close or flag — book via cal.com/ctoeast, or mark hot/cold. Recap to you.
Mode A — Owner-bridged triage (now)
You arm it with "ELSO <lead>", bridge the lead, listen in. East gives the hot/cold read. Hot → you call back to close. Cold → next lead. No extra infra.
Mode B — Full auto-bridge (AOD target)
Automation dials + bridges + injects the phrase; East books and only escalates hot. Gated on the Beside conference layer.
