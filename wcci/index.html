New Here?

Plan your first visit.

Walking through our doors for the first time should feel like coming home. Here's exactly what to expect when you join us at the World Dome.

1

Tell us you're coming

Let our team know to expect you — we'll have a friendly face ready to welcome you and answer questions.

2

Arrive a few minutes early

Free parking surrounds the World Dome. Greeters will point you to worship, kids' check-in, and guest services.

3

Experience worship

Sundays at 10 AM EST. Come as you are — expect uplifting praise and the uncompromised Word of God.

4

Take your next step

Connect with a member of our team, explore the Grace Gang, or join WCCI and get planted.

Who We Are

From 8 people to the nations.

World Changers Church International began in 1986 with just eight people gathered in a school cafeteria. Under the leadership of Senior Pastors Dr. Creflo Dollar and Taffi Dollar, WCCI has grown into a global ministry — with campuses across Georgia and offices in the UK, South Africa, Australia, and India.
