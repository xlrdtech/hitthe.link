Marina Abramović has lived an extraordinary life.
Dedicating her existence to art, her works focus not on physical
objects, but a transfer of energy: from the elusive chemistry
between people to the passing of time.
 
It's these moments of connection that the artist wants to
immortalize, doing so here by preserving five objects into an
immersive digital time capsule.
