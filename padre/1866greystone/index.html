Interpretation note
Standard NANP phone numbers are 10 digits (or 11 with leading 1). After "1-866-" only 7 digits dial. "GREYSTONE" is 9 letters, so phone systems read the first 7: GREYSTO → 473-9786. The "NE" trailing letters are vanity-only marketing.

If qi meant a specific Greystone business (Greystone Communications, Greystone Hotel, Greystone Manor, etc.), 1-866-473-9786 is the dialable line. Whether it's currently assigned to a specific Greystone-branded business requires a live carrier lookup (CNAM/411).
