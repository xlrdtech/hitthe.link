## The Hook (why this prospect)

Tobaz Towing already has the hard part handled: an established, FMCSA-registered local
reputation built since 2014 and 24/7 demand in a busy Clayton County corridor. The problem
is the front door. Their listed domain, `tobaztowingllc.com`, is dead — it serves a generic hosting
error page over HTTP and throws a TLS certificate error over HTTPS. So every driver who
Googles them, taps the website link, and lands on a broken page is a paying tow that
quietly went to a competitor instead of the phone.

**Angle:** "You've got a page out there, but it's not pulling its weight." This is not a
"you need a website" cold pitch — it's a "the one you have is broken and costing you
calls" rescue. The fix is a fast, mobile-first site that turns that broken link into a
tap-to-call machine, built and ready to show today.
