**"I already have a Facebook page / I'm on Angi."**
"That's exactly the issue — those aren't yours. Facebook owns the audience, Angi sells the same lead to your competitors, and you can't be found on Google through them the way you can with your own site. This site is one place you control completely. Keep the Facebook page — this just gives you a home base it can point to."

**"How much / what's the catch?"**
"$1,500, one time. $750 to start, the rest when it goes live. No monthly fee, no per-lead charge, no contract. You own the site and the domain outright."

**"I don't have time for this."**
"That's the best part — it's already built. I did the work up front. All I need from you is a few real photos and your actual reviews to swap in, and we handle the rest. You're looking at minutes of your time, not hours."

**"Do I really need a website? My phone already rings."**
"Good — that means demand's there. The question is how many calls you're missing. When someone searches 'lawn care McDonough' at night and you're not on Google with your own site, that call goes to whoever is. This makes sure it's you."

**"Let me think about it."**
"Totally fair. The demo stays live so you can show your spouse or business partner. Can I check back Thursday? And while you're thinking — pull it up on your phone one more time and imagine that's the first thing a new customer sees."

**"Can you change the reviews / those aren't my customers."**
"Correct — those are clearly-marked representative samples, not attributed to any real customer, and they're meant to be swapped. The moment you're in, we replace every one with your real reviews and real customer names. Nothing fake or falsely attributed goes live."
