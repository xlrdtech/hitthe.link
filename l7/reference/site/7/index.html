What L7S is — and isn't.

Do you guarantee a credit score increase or a funding amount?

No. We guarantee our work and process, never a specific outcome. Results vary based on your individual circumstances, the accuracy of your reports, and creditor/lender decisions. You also have the right to dispute inaccurate information yourself, for free, directly with the credit bureaus — and we cannot remove accurate negative information.

Are you a lender, credit-repair company, or financial advisor?

No. L7S is a strategy, education, and connection ecosystem. We provide frameworks and route members to licensed third-party professionals who deliver any regulated services.

Why the $39/mo plus one-time unlocks?

The membership keeps you in the community and core resources; one-time unlocks are permanent program access you buy only if and when you want them. No hidden recurring traps — everything is stated on the pricing above.

What happens on the fit call?

A 20-minute conversation to see whether L7S is the right fit for where you are — grounded in your Growth-Readiness Score. No pressure; if it's not a fit, we'll say so.
