Preamble & Recitals

This Partnership & Affiliate Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date set forth above by and between L7S Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Georgia, with its principal place of business at 760 Old Roswell Rd, Suite 324, Roswell, GA 30076 ("L7S," "Company," "we," or "us"), and the individual or entity that completes and is accepted into the L7S Partnership Program ("Partner," "Affiliate," or "you"). L7S and Partner are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

The Parties acknowledge the following:

L7S operates a strategic-planning, consulting, and coordination apparatus that provides productivity tools, digital products, planners, trackers, analytics, blueprints, and checklists, and that coordinates connections between its members and a network of independent, licensed third-party professionals.
L7S does not itself perform credit repair, tax, legal, financial-advisory, investment-advisory, lending, debt-collection, or insurance-brokerage services; such regulated services, where applicable, are performed exclusively by the licensed third-party professionals to whom members may be connected.
Partner wishes to refer prospective members to L7S and to earn referral compensation, and L7S wishes to appoint Partner on a non-exclusive basis, all on the terms set out below.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants below, the Parties agree as follows.

Section 01

Parties & Definitions

This Agreement is between L7S Inc. and Partner, as identified in the Preamble and in the signature blocks below. As used in this Agreement, the following terms have the meanings set forth here:

  1. "Affiliate Link" means the unique tracked URL, code, or referral mechanism that L7S issues to Partner for the purpose of attributing Referrals to Partner.
  2. "Attribution Window" means the cookie / tracking period defined in Section 4 during which a Qualified Lead's activity is credited to Partner's Affiliate Link.
  3. "Referral" means a prospective member or customer directed to L7S by Partner through Partner's Affiliate Link or other approved referral method.
  4. "Qualified Lead" means a Referral that meets L7S's then-current intake and qualification criteria.
  5. "Closed Transaction" means a Qualified Lead who enrolls in, purchases, and pays in full for an eligible L7S program or product, with all applicable refund or cancellation periods having expired.
  6. "Licensed Third-Party Professionals" means the independent credit professionals, law firms, accountants, financial advisors, and monitoring partners in L7S's coordinated network, none of whom are employees or agents of L7S.
  7. "Program" means the L7S Partnership Program governed by this Agreement, including its dashboard, terms, and any program policies L7S publishes from time to time.
Section 02

Appointment; Non-Exclusivity

2.1 Non-Exclusive Appointment. L7S appoints Partner as a non-exclusive, independent affiliate and referral partner for the limited purpose of referring prospective members to L7S in accordance with this Agreement. This Agreement does not create an exclusive arrangement between Partner and L7S. Each Party may enter into similar agreements with, recommend, or promote other parties, including parties that compete with the other, and nothing in this Agreement restricts either Party from doing so.

2.2 License Grant. Subject to this Agreement, L7S grants Partner a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use the Affiliate Link and L7S-approved promotional materials solely to promote and refer prospective members to L7S during the Term.

2.3 Enrollment & Acceptance. Partner's participation begins only upon L7S's written acceptance of Partner's completed application. L7S will review applications within thirty (30) days of submission and may accept or reject any applicant in its sole discretion. If L7S does not complete enrollment, or if Partner does not complete required enrollment criteria, within thirty (30) days, the application may be deemed terminated.

2.4 Excluded Persons. Current and former employees, contractors, officers, and directors of L7S, and members of their immediate households, are not eligible to earn compensation under this Agreement. Self-referrals and purchases through Partner's own Affiliate Link for Partner's own benefit are not eligible for compensation.

2.5 No Authority to Bind. Partner has no authority to bind L7S, to make representations on L7S's behalf, to set pricing, or to enter into agreements with prospective members on behalf of L7S. Partner refers; L7S coordinates and contracts directly with members.

Section 03

Commission & Referral Fees

L7S compensates Partner on Closed Transactions originated by Partner. The Program uses a tiered, volume-based referral-fee structure modeled on standard field practice for referral / channel-partner programs in the business-consulting and funding-coordination space. The applicable schedule is:

3.1 — Referral-Fee Schedule
TierQualifying Volume (rolling 12 mo.)Referral Fee per Closed Transaction
SILVER1–9 Closed Transactions20% of net program fee
GOLD10–24 Closed Transactions25% of net program fee
PLATINUM25+ Closed Transactions30% of net program fee
QUALIFIED LEADPer Qualified Lead (where offered)$25 flat (CPL)

3.2 Net Program Fee. "Net program fee" means the amount actually collected and retained by L7S for the eligible program or product, excluding taxes, processing fees, refunds, chargebacks, and any amounts paid through to Licensed Third-Party Professionals.

3.3 Eligible Earnings Only. Referral fees are earned only on the strategic-planning, consulting, coordination, and digital-product offerings sold by L7S. Partner does not earn, and L7S does not pay, any commission, fee, split, or origination percentage on credit-repair outcomes, loan or funding amounts, tax results, investment returns, insurance products, or any service performed by a Licensed Third-Party Professional. Compensation is for the referral of members to L7S's lawful, non-regulated offerings only.

Compliance Note

Referral fees are tied to L7S program enrollments, not to any financial, credit, lending, tax, or investment outcome. No compensation is contingent on, or measured by, any score, funding amount, dollar saved, or other regulated result.

3.4 Changes to Schedule. L7S may modify the Referral-Fee Schedule prospectively upon thirty (30) days' written notice (including by email or dashboard notice). Changes do not affect referral fees already earned on Closed Transactions occurring before the change takes effect.

Section 04

Payment Terms & Attribution

4.1 Attribution / Cookie Window. A Referral is attributed to Partner when the prospective member arrives through Partner's Affiliate Link and a tracking cookie is placed. The Attribution Window is ninety (90) days from the click. If a prospect becomes a Closed Transaction within the Attribution Window, the transaction is credited to Partner, subject to last-click attribution where multiple affiliates are involved.

4.2 Payout Cadence. Earned referral fees are calculated and paid monthly, on or about the fifteenth (15th) day of the month following the month in which the Closed Transaction's refund / cancellation period expired.

4.3 Minimum Payout Threshold. L7S pays out once Partner's accrued, earned balance reaches a minimum of $50.00 USD. Balances below the threshold roll forward and accumulate until the threshold is met.

4.4 Clawback / Reversal. Referral fees on transactions later refunded, charged back, cancelled, or determined to be fraudulent or non-compliant will be reversed and deducted from current or future payouts.

4.5 Tax Information. Partner is responsible for providing a valid Form W-9 (or W-8, as applicable) and accurate payment information. L7S may withhold payment until required tax documentation is received and may issue a Form 1099 where required by law.

4.6 Dashboard & Records. L7S provides a partner dashboard or reporting mechanism reflecting tracked Referrals, Closed Transactions, and accrued fees. L7S's records are the controlling record of amounts owed, absent manifest error.

Section 05

Term & Termination

5.1 Term. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date (or L7S's acceptance of Partner's application, if later) and continues until terminated as provided here.

5.2 Termination for Convenience. Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon fifteen (15) days' written notice (which may be given by email or dashboard notice).

5.3 Termination for Cause. L7S may suspend or terminate Partner immediately, without notice, if Partner breaches this Agreement; engages in unlawful, deceptive, spam, or non-compliant promotion; misuses L7S marks or the Affiliate Link; or makes any prohibited guarantee or regulated representation.

5.4 Effect of Termination. Upon termination, the license in Section 2.2 ends, Partner must cease all use of L7S marks and the Affiliate Link, and Partner is entitled to referral fees earned on Closed Transactions occurring on or before the termination date, less any reversals, paid in the ordinary payout cycle. No referral fee accrues on transactions after termination.

5.5 Survival. Sections 6 through 15 (and any provision that by its nature should survive) survive termination.

Section 06

FTC Affiliate Disclosure

6.1 Mandatory Disclosure. Partner must clearly and conspicuously disclose its material connection to L7S in every promotion, post, page, message, video, or communication that could earn Partner a referral fee, in accordance with the FTC's Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising, 16 CFR Part 255.

6.2 Form of Disclosure. The disclosure must be placed where the audience will readily see it (not buried behind a link or in a hover), in plain language, before any call to action — for example: "I may earn a referral fee if you enroll with L7S through my link."

6.3 Truthful, Substantiated Claims. Partner may make only truthful, non-misleading statements about L7S and must not misrepresent L7S, its offerings, or the role of the Licensed Third-Party Professionals. Partner may not state or imply that L7S performs regulated services or guarantees any outcome.

6.4 Responsibility. Partner is solely responsible for the legality and accuracy of its own marketing and for complying with the FTC Guides, the CAN-SPAM Act, the TCPA, applicable platform rules, and all other applicable advertising laws.

Section 07

Trademark & Intellectual Property

7.1 Ownership. L7S owns all right, title, and interest in the L7S name, the "Hero S.O.S." wordmark, logos, the F.O.O.D.I.E.S.™ and W.O.M.E.N.™ designations, the R&R™ framework, copy, graphics, and all related intellectual property (collectively, the "L7S Marks"). No ownership transfers to Partner.

7.2 Limited License to Use Marks. During the Term, Partner may use the L7S Marks and L7S-approved promotional materials solely to promote referrals under this Agreement, strictly in accordance with any brand guidelines L7S provides.

7.3 Restrictions. Partner shall not: (a) modify the L7S Marks; (b) register or bid on the L7S Marks (or confusingly similar terms) as domain names, social handles, or paid-search keywords without L7S's prior written consent; (c) imply partnership, agency, employment, joint venture, or endorsement beyond the referral relationship described here; or (d) use the L7S Marks in any unlawful, deceptive, or disparaging manner.

7.4 Reservation. All rights not expressly granted are reserved by L7S. The license terminates automatically on termination of this Agreement.

Section 08

Confidentiality

8.1 Confidential Information. Each Party may receive non-public information of the other, including program terms, pricing, payout data, member and prospect information, frameworks, and business methods ("Confidential Information").

8.2 Obligations. The receiving Party shall (a) use Confidential Information solely to perform under this Agreement, (b) protect it with at least reasonable care, and (c) not disclose it to third parties except to those with a need to know who are bound by similar obligations.

8.3 Personal Data. Partner shall handle any prospect or member personal data lawfully, shall not sell or misuse it, and shall comply with applicable privacy laws and L7S's privacy policy.

8.4 Exclusions & Survival. Confidentiality does not apply to information that is public through no fault of the receiving Party, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law (with notice where permitted). These obligations survive for two (2) years after termination, and indefinitely for trade secrets and personal data.

Section 09

Independent Contractor

9.1 Relationship. Partner is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, agency, partnership, joint venture, or franchise relationship between the Parties.

9.2 No Benefits or Withholding. Partner is not entitled to employee benefits and is solely responsible for its own taxes, expenses, insurance, equipment, and personnel. L7S will not withhold taxes from referral fees.

9.3 Control. Partner controls the manner and means of its own promotional activities, subject to the lawful and compliance limits in this Agreement.

Section 10

Compliance with Law (CROA / FTC)

10.1 L7S Role — Coordination, Not Regulated Services. Partner acknowledges and agrees that L7S is a strategic-planning, consulting, and coordination firm. L7S coordinates and connects members to independent, Licensed Third-Party Professionals and does not itself perform credit repair, lending, tax, legal, financial-advisory, investment-advisory, debt-collection, or insurance services. The Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA, 15 U.S.C. § 1679 et seq.) and similar laws govern regulated providers; the regulated work, where applicable, is performed solely by those licensed providers.

10.2 Partner Compliance. Partner shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations, including the FTC Act and the FTC Endorsement Guides (16 CFR Part 255), CROA, the Telemarketing Sales Rule, CAN-SPAM, and the TCPA. Partner shall not market L7S as a credit-repair, lending, tax, legal, investment, or insurance provider.

10.3 Prohibited Representations. Partner shall not promise, guarantee, or imply any specific credit score increase, funding or loan amount, approval, tax result, investment return, debt elimination, or other regulated outcome in connection with L7S.

10.4 Indemnity for Non-Compliance. Partner shall indemnify and hold harmless L7S from claims, losses, and expenses arising out of Partner's marketing, misrepresentations, or violation of law or this Agreement.

Section 11

No Guarantee of Results

11.1 No Outcome Guarantee. L7S cannot and does not guarantee specific outcomes or results from the use of its services. Results will vary depending on individual circumstances. The information and tools provided are for educational and informational purposes only and do not constitute professional advice.

11.2 No Earnings Guarantee. L7S makes no representation or guarantee as to the amount Partner may earn under this Agreement. Any commission ranges, tiers, or figures are illustrative of the Program's structure and field-standard practice, are not promises of income, and depend entirely on Partner's own results, volume, and compliance.

11.3 Partner Conduct. Partner shall not, directly or indirectly, make any earnings, income, or outcome guarantee to prospective members on L7S's behalf.

Section 12

Limitation of Liability

12.1 No Indirect Damages. To the maximum extent permitted by law, neither Party is liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special, exemplary, or punitive damages, or for lost profits, revenue, data, or goodwill, arising out of or relating to this Agreement, even if advised of the possibility.

12.2 Liability Cap. L7S's aggregate liability arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall not exceed the total referral fees paid by L7S to Partner in the six (6) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

12.3 Third-Party Services. L7S is not responsible or liable for the acts, omissions, advice, or services of any Licensed Third-Party Professional, all of whom are independent of L7S.

12.4 Basis of the Bargain. These limitations are an essential basis of the bargain and apply notwithstanding the failure of any limited remedy.

Section 13

Non-Disparagement

13.1 Mutual Non-Disparagement. During the Term and for two (2) years after termination, neither Party shall make, publish, or encourage any false, misleading, or disparaging statement about the other Party, its officers, services, or the Program.

13.2 Preserved Rights. Nothing in this Section restricts either Party from making truthful statements required by law, to a regulator, or in good faith to enforce or defend its legal rights, or from filing a complaint with a governmental agency.

Section 14

Governing Law & Venue

14.1 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Georgia, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.

14.2 Venue. The Parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction and venue of the state and federal courts located in Fulton County, Georgia, for any dispute not otherwise resolved, and waive any objection to that venue.

14.3 Equitable Relief. Either Party may seek injunctive or equitable relief to protect its intellectual property or Confidential Information without posting a bond.

Section 15

Entire Agreement; Miscellaneous

15.1 Entire Agreement. This Agreement, together with any program policies and the application L7S accepts, is the entire agreement between the Parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous understandings, whether written or oral.

15.2 Amendments. Except for prospective Program changes expressly permitted in this Agreement (e.g., Section 3.4), any amendment must be in writing.

15.3 Assignment. Partner may not assign this Agreement without L7S's prior written consent. L7S may assign it to an affiliate or successor.

15.4 Severability. If any provision is held unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force, and the unenforceable provision will be reformed to the minimum extent necessary.

15.5 Waiver. No waiver is effective unless in writing, and no waiver of a breach waives any other or subsequent breach.

15.6 Notices. Notices to L7S go to admin@l7sinc.com and the address in the Preamble; notices to Partner go to the contact information in Partner's application. Email and dashboard notices are sufficient where this Agreement so provides.

15.7 Force Majeure. Neither Party is liable for delay or failure to perform due to causes beyond its reasonable control.

15.8 Counterparts & E-Signature. This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature, each of which is an original and together one instrument.
