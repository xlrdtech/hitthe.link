How allocation works

Your subscription sets a monthly quota (30 or 15). At the start of each billing period, Eli fills your account up to that quota from the qualified lead pool. Each lead is yours and visible only to you, enforced by Supabase Row-Level Security. Billing, cancellation, and card changes are handled by Stripe's hosted Customer Portal.
