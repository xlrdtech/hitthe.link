Overview

Your lead portal

A private, always-on home for the Tracking Together leads. It lives in the cloud (not on anyone's laptop), so it is up around the clock. Masters see and manage every lead and hand specific ones to subscribers; each subscriber sees only the leads assigned to them. Every login is locked to its own account at the database level.

Getting started

1
Open hitthe.link/elios on any phone or computer (it also installs to a home screen like an app).
2
Sign in with the username and password sent to you privately. Masters land on the command console; subscribers on their own dashboard.
3
Masters: tick the leads you want a rep to have, pick the rep, and press Assign. That rep sees them immediately.
4
Light or dark mode is your choice — toggle in the top-right, it remembers your preference.
