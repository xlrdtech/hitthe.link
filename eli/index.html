II · What Eli Does

Three things, done well

lead generation · the value-block model · the buyer portal
🎯

Qualified lead generation

Leads are only worth what they convert to. Eli takes home-improvement interest and turns it into qualified, contactable records: sorted by stage, cleaned of duplicates, and ready for a buyer to actually work. Volume that means something.

🧱

The value-block model

Access is packaged in value blocks: a defined volume of qualified leads delivered on a clear cadence, at a clear price. Buyers know exactly what they get, sellers run a predictable book, and tiers flex up or down as the relationship grows.

🔐

The EliOS buyer portal

Every buyer gets a private, secure sign-in to a branded portal, wired to checkout so paying for access and getting access are one smooth step. No shared logins, no loose links, no leakage. Real access for real buyers.

III · The Value Block

Why blocks beat raw lists

predictable for the buyer, scalable for the seller

A raw list is a gamble. A value block is a product. The buyer subscribes to a set volume of qualified, contactable leads, delivered on a schedule, with a private portal login that proves they are paying for something real.

It makes the seller side calm and the buyer side confident. Volumes and prices are starting points, not locks: tiers move with the buyer, the cadence adapts, and the portal stays the single source of truth either way.
