Forex Entry / Exit System
\n

London Day Break

\n
Curtis Henderson's GBP-cross breakout playbook. Price the 2-3 AM London open, place an OCO pair of pending orders, and let the market pick a side — sized to the dollar.
\n
\n\n
\n\n
\n
01

Strategy Rules

per Curtis
\n
\n
    \n
  1. Mark the 2 AM candle high and low (London pre-open).
    2. \n
  2. Place a BUY-STOP 10 pips above the high.
    3. \n
  3. Place a SELL-STOP 5 pips below the low.
    4. \n
  4. Stop loss: 30 pips from entry. Take profit: 25 pips (or 20 if previous candles were large).
    5. \n
  5. Once one side triggers, cancel the opposite order immediately (OCO).
    6. \n
